Carpool Management System for Students

Developed a comprehensive Carpool Management System tailored for students seeking efficient and free transportation within the city. This intuitive platform offers a dual-functionality interface: riders can easily register and manage their journeys, while those in need of a ride can book and track their trips in real-time.
